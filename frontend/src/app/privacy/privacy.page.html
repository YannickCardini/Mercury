Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 15, 2025

1. Introduction

Mercury ("the App") is a multiplayer board game developed by Yannick Cardini. This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, why we collect it, and how you can control it. By using the App you agree to this policy.

2. Data We Collect

When you sign in with Google, we receive and store:

  • Name — your Google display name (you can change it in the App).
  • Profile picture — your Google profile photo (you can replace it in the App).
  • Email address — used only to identify your account; never displayed to other players.
  • Google account ID — a unique identifier used to link your account securely.

During normal use we also store:

  • Game statistics — points earned, ranking position, and match history.
  • Session data — a temporary identifier that lets you resume a game if you disconnect.

If you play as a guest (without signing in), only an anonymous session identifier is used. It is not linked to any personal information and is cleared when you close the App.

3. How We Use Your Data

  • To create and manage your account.
  • To match you with other players and enable multiplayer games.
  • To maintain the leaderboard and display your rank.
  • To let other players send you game invitations.
  • To restore your session if you disconnect mid-game.

We do not use your data for advertising, sell it to third parties, or share it with anyone outside the App.

4. Google Sign-In

Authentication is handled by Google Sign-In. When you sign in, Google shares basic profile information with us (name, email, profile picture, and account ID) as permitted by the OAuth 2.0 scope. We do not request access to your contacts, calendar, or any other Google services. Google's own Privacy Policy governs how Google handles your data.

5. Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored on secure cloud servers. We take reasonable technical measures to protect it from unauthorized access. Game session data that is no longer needed is periodically removed.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you.
  • Correct inaccurate data — you can update your name and profile picture directly in the App.
  • Delete your account and all associated data.
  • Object to or restrict certain types of processing.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at yannick.cardini@gmail.com. We will respond within 30 days.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted in the App with an updated date at the top of this page.
