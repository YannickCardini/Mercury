## BUG: /home page layout clips the footer at default zoom

**Problem:**
The `/home` route cuts off the footer (the version/settings button) at 100% browser zoom.
The user has to zoom out to 90% to see the full page. The `/game` route is not affected.

**Root cause:**
In `frontend/src/app/home/home.page.scss`, `.home-content` sets `overflow: hidden` and
`.home-wrap` uses `position: absolute; inset: 0; overflow: hidden`. The three flex children
(`.home-nav`, `.home-hero`, `.home-footer`) cannot all fit vertically on short/standard
viewports, so the footer is clipped.

**Steps to fix:**
1. Open `frontend/src/app/home/home.page.scss`.
2. Make `.home-hero` shrinkable by adding `flex-shrink: 1` and `overflow: hidden` (or
   `overflow-y: auto`) on it, while ensuring `.home-nav` and `.home-footer` have
   `flex-shrink: 0` so they are never clipped.
3. Verify the fix: at 100% zoom, both the nav bar at the top and the settings/version
   button in the footer must be fully visible without scrolling.
4. Check that the orbit decoration and action buttons inside `.home-hero` still render
   correctly and are not cropped after the fix.

## BUG: Active sounds are not stopped when the player resigns

**Problem:**
When the local human player resigns (abandons the game), any currently playing sounds
(e.g. the `playNewTurn` bell, which lasts ~1.2s, or `playCountdownTick` ticks) continue
playing until they naturally decay. There is no mechanism to silence the `AudioContext`.

**Relevant files:**
- `frontend/src/app/game/services/sound.service.ts` ÔÇö all sound synthesis and playback
- `frontend/src/app/game/services/game-state.service.ts` ÔÇö `sendAbandonGame()` at line 420

**Steps to fix:**

1. **Add a `stopAll()` method to `SoundService`**
   - The cleanest approach is to suspend (or close and nullify) the `AudioContext`:
     ```ts
     stopAll(): void {
       this.ctx?.suspend();
     }
     ```
   - `suspend()` immediately stops all scheduled audio and is reversible via `resume()`
     (which `getCtx()` already calls). This ensures future sounds after a new game still
     work correctly.
   - An alternative (more aggressive) is `this.ctx?.close()` followed by
     `this.ctx = null`, which also works since `getCtx()` recreates the context on demand.

2. **Call `stopAll()` when the player resigns**
   - In `GamePage` (`frontend/src/app/game/game.page.ts`), find the location where resign
     is triggered (the UI button that calls `gameStateService.sendAbandonGame()`).
   - Call `soundService.stopAll()` immediately before or after `sendAbandonGame()`.
   - Alternatively, call `stopAll()` inside `sendAbandonGame()` in `GameStateService` if
     `SoundService` is injected there (currently it is not; prefer calling it from
     `GamePage` where `SoundService` is already injected).

3. **Verify the fix**
   - Start a game, wait for a new-turn bell to begin playing, then resign immediately.
   - Confirm the sound stops at the moment of resignation.
   - Start another game immediately after and confirm sounds play normally.
